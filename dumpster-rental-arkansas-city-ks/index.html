Frequently Asked Questions

What size roll off dumpster do I need for my project?

The right roll off dumpster size hinges on the scale of your project. A 10 yard roll off dumpster is generally adequate for minor cleanouts or small renovations. The 20 yard roll off dumpster is a popular option for more extensive residential projects, like kitchen or bathroom remodels. For significant construction or demolition work, a 30 yard roll off dumpster or a 40 yard roll off dumpster is recommended. Our team is available to help you evaluate your requirements and suggest the ideal rolloff sizes for your task.

How much does a roll off dumpster rental cost?

The roll off dumpster cost is influenced by several factors, such as the dumpster's size, the duration of the rental, the nature of the debris you're disposing of, and your location. We provide transparent roll off dumpster rental prices without any hidden charges. For an accurate quote, please reach out to us, and we'll gladly provide you with a comprehensive estimate.

What can I put in a roll off dumpster?

Our roll off dumpsters in Arkansas City, KS are suitable for a broad array of materials, such as household clutter, construction waste, yard debris, and old furniture. However, certain hazardous materials are prohibited, including chemicals, paint, oil, and asbestos. Kindly inform us about the type of materials you intend to dispose of so we can inform you of any relevant guidelines.

How long can I keep the dumpster rental?

Our typical rental period ranges from 7 to 14 days. We also offer adaptable rental arrangements to suit your project's specific timeline. If you require the roll off rental for a different duration, we can collaborate with you to set up a personalized rental plan.
